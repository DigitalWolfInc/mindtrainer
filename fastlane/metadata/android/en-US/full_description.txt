Mind Trainer is a comprehensive mental training and mindfulness app designed to help you improve focus, memory, and overall well-being.

Key Features:
• Memory Training: Engaging exercises to enhance pattern recognition and recall
• Focus Training: Attention-building tasks with progressive difficulty
• Mindfulness Sessions: Guided breathing and meditation with calming visuals
• Progress Tracking: Detailed analytics and achievement system
• Emergency Calm: Quick access to grounding exercises when needed

Mind Trainer adapts to your skill level and provides a structured path to mental fitness. Track your progress, earn achievements, and build healthy daily habits.
